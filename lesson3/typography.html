Typography

Headings

All of the headings for OurWeatherHotSpot.com will be in the Rock Salt font. It's a fun, all caps font that is easy to read but draws your attention and makes it stand out.

Text

The rest of the text will be in the Handlee font. This is a handwriting font but one that reads almost like a sans serif font. It has great readability even when scaled down and compliments the Rock Salt headings.
