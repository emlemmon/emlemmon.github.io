Proposed Typography

Marcellus

For the headers of the Arvada Chamber of Commerce website I will be using the font Marcellus.

This font is a serif font with a thicker line. It is bold without being obnoxious and will serve my purposes well of bring attention to headers while being easy to read and professional looking as well.


Lato

The Lato font will be used for all blocks of text in the website. It is a sans serif font that is good for large or long blocks of text. It is readable without being boring.

The Lato font also has 10 different styles, thin, italized, bold, etc, which makes it very usable in different situations and for different feels in different sections while still presesnting a cohesive look to the site.
