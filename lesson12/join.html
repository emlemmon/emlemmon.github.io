Join Our Community

If you've been pondering becoming a member of the Arvada Chamber of Commerce, then we say, "Welcome!" We'd love to have you!
There are many reasons to join our chamber, but the first that comes to mind is the chance to be a part of a close-knit, supportive community. Arvada is a beautiful city with a lot of growth, and joining the Chamber of Commerce is a great way to be a part of that growth and action. There will be oppurtunities to get to know the other local business owners, networking and creating friendships and partnerships, as well as promote your business to the community at large.

Membership Levels Explained

  • Non-profit: For our non-profit level of membership, you'll have all the basic acces to our services, specificially inclusion in our directory, basic advertising on our website and the oppurtunity to be involved in community outreach programs.
  • Bronze: Our bronze level includes everything the previous level receives, as well as priority listings on our website, business training sessions, and social media advertising as well.
  • Silver: The silver level continues to build off of the Bronze level. It includes everything previously mentioned as well as extra advertising on our local radio station and 20 free tickets to the annual Arvada Businesses Social.
  • Gold: Our gold membership is the premier membership and comes with exclusive benefits including 30 Arvada Businesses Social tickets, employee training seminars, and first choice with sponsoring oppurtunities.
