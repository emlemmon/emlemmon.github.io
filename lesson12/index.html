All about our Chamber

The Chamber of Commerce in Arvada has been serving the businesses an residents here for over 35 years. Founded by Kat Johnsen, an Arvada native, when she was only 22, the Chamber has grown to become the most used business resource in the area. We support our citizens by supporting their companies. Our hands on approach to being there for every one of our members is said to be the best part of being a member of the Arvada Chamber of Commerce. We know you love Arvada as much as we do, and we'll always be here to help support our fiscal community.

Arvada History

The roots of Arvada, Colorado go back to June 22, 1850, when Lewis Ralston made the first documented discovery of gold in Colorado. Ralston didn’t stay to capitalize on his discovery, but in 1858, he guided a group of gold seekers back to the banks of the creek which now bore his name.
