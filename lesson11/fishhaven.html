Fish Haven Friends

By Emily Lemmon

For today's feature we are going to introduce you to some of Fish Haven's finest residents. These fair folk bring so much to our little town, so today the spotlight is on them.

Clair Annette Reed is first up in our list of rapturous residents. Having lived in Fish Haven for 15 years now, Clair brings music to all who know her. She sings as she surveys her garden, she plays the flute while she fishes in the lake, she hums while handling her happy hogs. Her musicality is appreciated by all her admirers. Though only standing at 4 feet 7 inches tall, her diminuative stature is no no way an indication of her presence in this town. To know Clair is to love Clair, everyone agrees.

Next up in our lovely list is Loretta Loques. Sweet Loretta is an impressive 104 years old. Known all around for her luscious locks, the children of Fish Haven fondly refer to her as Rapunzel. When loose, her hair brushes her ankles, but Loretta often wears it braided in a regal crown atop her head. Pure white from age, Loretta's hair glows in the gorgeous sunsets. Not only her hair sets her apart from the rest of the town, but her kindness and generosity as well. Loretta never goes anywhere without her satchel of treats, which she freely gives to anyone she comes across. Loretta is a town treasure.

Finally, no enumeration of individuals would be complete without mentioning young Jonathan Archer. Ever the explorer, Jonathan followed his father's dreams and was the first of Fish Haven to explore the new worlds of the mountains to our north. Finding new species is his speciality, and he is always willing to help those in need. Jonathan will fight for fairness if need be, and he is a natural leader for those around him. Nothing will keep Jonathan back, he's sure to go far in life!
