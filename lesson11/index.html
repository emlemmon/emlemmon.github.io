Falling for Idaho

By Emily Lemmon

I'll never forget the first time I visited Idaho. I was young, probably only 10 years old. The sky seemed to go on forever, and the open expanse beckoned me to run and get lost in the fields. I slept out on the trampoline that night with my cousins, staring at more stars than I had ever realized could be up in the night sky. The beauty of Idaho drew me in and from then on, I knew I wanted to come back as often as I could.

I came back every summer, and I fell more and more in love. I helped out at my cousins ranch, feeding the horses, mucking out the stalls, picking flowers and weeding the garden. Being in Idaho in the summers became the thing that I looked forward to all year. After high school, I started college at Ricks, just 20 minutes away from my cousins ranch that I worked on so often. It was a dream come true, and it didn't take me long to decide that I wanted to raise my family here and give them the happiness that I had had throughout all those childhood summers.

To experience Idaho is to love Idaho, and our little corner of Idaho is especially wonderful. Preston, Soda Springs, and quaint little Fish Haven call you to relax and enjoy yourself while living your best life out on the country. Get to know us, you'll agree, there is no better place on earth. Welcome to Beautiful Idaho!
