Stupendous Soda Springs Extravaganza

By Emily Lemmon

Come one, come all, to Soda Springs Idaho to experience the Stupendous Soda Springs Extravaganza! A one of a kind experience that you will find no where else, the Soda Springs Extravaganza is a sensational event sure to please the child within us all!

What can one find at the Soda Springs Extravaganza, you might ask? Why, only the most marvelous modern miracles known to Southern Idaho. Find oversized Potatoes, larger than you've ever seen! These delectable delicacies can be served in a myriad of ways. We have mashed potatoes, french fried potatoes, baked potatoes, potato soup, potato chowder, potato bread. Truly there is no greater vegetable than the humble potato from our fine town.

We don't stop there, no! Not by a long shot. Find as well various varieties of superb soda! We're not called Soda Springs for nothing, folks, as our Soda Springs Extravaganza is sure to prove to you. Sample such sodas as Sprite, Crush, Dr. Pepper, good ol' Coca Cola. Not adventurous enough, you say? Never fear, we also have locally grown Blackberry Blitz! Soda Springs specialty is made right here by long-time resident Barry Barton whose blackberry bushes have been in the family for 4 generations!

Don't forget to stop by our Donut Drop-off to be a part of the taste test to determine Soda Springs most delicious deep fried donut! There's sure to be Apple Fritters, Maple Bars, Chocolate Crinkles, Lemon Filled, Cream Filled, Crumb Cake, you name it, we'll have it! Don't miss out on this highly anticipated Soda Springs Extravaganza yearly tradition! We can't wait to see you there!
